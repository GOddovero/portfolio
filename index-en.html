Chefcito

Final project for the Bachelor's Degree in Informatics. The goal of the project was to include artificial intelligence to improve a process, with the chosen process being RECIPES and how they are searched. Traditionally, you first search for the recipe and then see the ingredients needed. In the case of "Chefcito," it's the other way around. The ingredients the user possesses are added to a database, and an API from Groq is used to relate them to generate recipes. Additionally, Dall-e was used to generate images.
